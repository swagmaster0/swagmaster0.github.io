Deterioration

Released 23 December 2024

In this roguelike survival game, you must seek out power to keep your salvation alive. Survive in this hellscape for as long as possible.



This game was made just after Amelioration for Godot Wild Jam #76. With this game, I wanted to try C# programming. I was sick of Godot's GDScript because of it's exclusivity to Godot, and wanted to try something more universal and widespread.

In hindsight, this could have been better. The game's visuals were over-stylised and hard to decern, and some gameplay elements were frustrating to deal with. Otherwise, I learned a lot from this project - which then led to the beginning of Sunrise.
